Expressions:-

An expression is a combination of values, variables, and operators, which computes to a value.
Example:- !true => false;

Operators:-

They are used to perform some operations between two or more operands.

1. Arithmetic Operators-
+, -, *, /, **, %, ++, --

2. Assignment Operators-
=, +=, -=, *=, /=, 

3. Comparison Operator
==, !=, ===, !==, <, >, <=, >=, ?(Ternary Operator)

4. Logical Operator
&&, ||, !

5. Bitwise Operator
